Meet Sarita Avasthy ..

Welcome to Parivartan! As a compassionate, dedicated, and collaborative psychological counsellor, my mission is to provide an emotionally safe and supportive environment for every individual who walks through my doors.

With extensive experience working alongside diverse age groups, I have had the privilege of assisting parents in understanding their children better and helping clients navigate complex emotional landscapes. My practice provides dedicated support for those facing anxiety, depression, stress, grief, phobias, OCD, poor coping skills, and dysfunctional behavior patterns.

Beyond clinical challenges, I also provide structured support in personality development, career counselling, and breaking down communication barriers. Together, we can work towards healing, clarity, and sustainable personal growth.

Professional Experience

Educational Institutions

Served extensively as a Senior Psychological Counsellor, guiding students through academic stress, peer pressure, and behavioral challenges while assisting parents in holistic child development.

Corporate Sector Wellness

Collaborated with leading corporate sectors to conduct employee wellness programs, focusing on managing burnout, workplace anxiety, and maintaining a healthy work-life balance.

Private Therapeutic Practice

Facilitated hundreds of successful family, adolescent, and individual therapy sessions specializing in behavioral interventions and personalized developmental action plans.

My Counselling Style

Every individual’s journey is unique. My practice is grounded in a humanistic and empathetic approach, designed to foster trust and facilitate deep, meaningful transformation.

Humanistic Approach

Focusing on empathy, warmth, and genuineness to create a non-judgmental space.

Thought Process Focus

Helping clients understand their emotional responses and cognitive patterns.

Root Cause Discovery

Looking beneath the surface symptoms to address the core of the distress.

Self-Acceptance

Encouraging radical self-acceptance as the foundation for positive change.

Client Empowerment

Guiding clients to discover and implement their own sustainable solutions.

Positive Transformation

Moving from a state of coping to a state of thriving and well-being.
